# Executive Summary

## Project

**Financial Performance Analysis: Budget vs Actual Variance and Revenue Forecasting Dashboard**

## Objective

The objective of this project is to evaluate FY2025 financial performance for a fictional business unit, identify major budget vs actual variances, forecast future performance, and provide management recommendations.

## Financial Performance

FY2025 actual revenue was **$14,634,571**, compared with budget revenue of **$14,474,470**. This resulted in a favorable revenue variance of **$160,101**, or **1.1%**.

Actual operating profit was **$4,210,653**, compared with budget operating profit of **$4,171,342**. This created a favorable profit variance of **$39,311**.

Actual operating margin was **28.8%**.

## Key Insights

1. Revenue finished above budget due to stronger Q4 demand and upsell momentum.
2. Profitability was positive overall, but **Jun-2025** had the weakest profit variance.
3. Marketing and salary costs are the main controllable expense areas to monitor.
4. The business should protect high-ROI campaigns while cutting low-converting spend.

## Management Recommendations

- Continue high-performing upsell and enterprise renewal campaigns.
- Review campaign ROI before approving additional marketing spend.
- Monitor hiring pace and salary growth against revenue growth.
- Use the forecast model monthly to update base, best, and worst-case scenarios.
