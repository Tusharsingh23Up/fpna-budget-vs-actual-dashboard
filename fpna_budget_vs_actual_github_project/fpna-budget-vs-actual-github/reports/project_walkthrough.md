# Project Walkthrough

## Step 1: Understand the Problem

The business needs to compare actual financial performance against budget and identify why performance changed.

## Step 2: Prepare the Dataset

The dataset includes monthly budget and actual values for revenue, COGS, salary expense, marketing expense, operations expense, and admin expense.

## Step 3: Calculate KPIs

Calculated KPIs include:

- Revenue variance
- Revenue variance %
- Gross profit
- Total operating expense
- Operating profit
- Profit variance
- Operating margin
- Monthly revenue growth

## Step 4: Analyze Variance Drivers

Each month has a driver note explaining why performance was above or below plan.

## Step 5: Build Forecast

The Excel model uses editable assumptions for:

- Base-case growth
- Best-case growth
- Worst-case growth
- COGS as % of revenue
- Opex as % of revenue

## Step 6: Build Dashboard

The dashboard summarizes:

- Revenue performance
- Operating profit performance
- Expense variance
- Forecast revenue and profit
- Executive recommendations

## Step 7: Present Recommendations

The final recommendation focuses on growing revenue through high-ROI channels while controlling marketing and hiring expenses.
